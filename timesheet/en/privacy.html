Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 14, 2024

1. Information Collection

This website (Freelancer Income Calculate and Timesheet Export) is a pure frontend application. All calculations are performed locally in your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal information or calculation data.

2. Local Storage

To enhance user experience, this website may store the following information locally in your browser:

  • Theme Preference: Your selected dark/light mode setting
  • Holiday Data Cache: Cached data for quick loading of holiday information

This data is stored only on your device and is not sent to any server.

3. Third-Party Services

This website uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics: Used to analyze website traffic and help us understand how users interact with this website. Google Analytics may collect anonymous information such as your IP address, browser type, and visit time. You can visit Google's Privacy Policy for more information.
  • GitHub: Used to load holiday data. We only read data from public GitHub repositories and do not send any personal information to GitHub.

4. Cookie Usage

This website may use cookies and similar technologies to store your preference settings. These cookies are only used to improve user experience and are not used to track or identify you.

5. Data Security

Since all calculations are performed locally in your browser, your calculation data never leaves your device. We do not store, transmit, or access any of your calculation data.

6. Children's Privacy

This website is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Privacy Policy Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page and the "Last Updated" date will be revised. We recommend that you review this page periodically for the latest information.
